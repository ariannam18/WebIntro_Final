5/5/ - I'm somehow here at least 2-3 times a week usually for dinner. Located near the university of Miami, the lines can get pretty long usually between 8PM-8:45 on weekdays. Coming here after practice is a tradition for the table tennis team on most days. These long lines are worth the abnormal amount of food this location seems to give. The veteran servers are polite but distant enough to keep their cool over the kind of University crowd that occasionally gives them trouble. The food and quality are as consistent as it gets for this kind of restaurant model. Plenty of tables at this location and outdoor seating. There are two raised bars that seat 6 and a round table, so larger parties should keep that in mind. The staff are also mindful of return customers and I have received the occasional complementary drink or even a free bowl for continued loyalty to this restaurant. I come back anyway though as you can't find this much satisfactory food at a relatively low price save for cooking on your own! For those who aren't super picky about hyper authentic Mexican cuisine, you just cannot pass up this location. - Justin M.
